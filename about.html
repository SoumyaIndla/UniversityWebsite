About Our College

Annamacharya University is a well-known educational institution that focuses on providing quality education to students. The university offers various undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering, science, and management.

The campus has modern infrastructure including laboratories, computer centers, libraries, and classrooms. Students receive guidance from experienced faculty members who help them gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

The university encourages students to participate in technical events, seminars, workshops, and research activities. These programs help students improve their technical knowledge and develop innovative ideas.

Apart from academics, the university also promotes sports and cultural activities. Students participate in competitions, sports tournaments, and cultural festivals which help them develop confidence and teamwork skills.

The placement cell supports students in getting jobs in well-known companies. Many companies visit the campus every year to recruit talented students.

With its focus on education, research, and skill development, Annamacharya University aims to prepare students for successful careers and responsible citizenship.
